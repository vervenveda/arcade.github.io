How to Play

  1. Press Start. Balloons float left ➜ right.
  2. Drag the Shield to block hazards (time slows slightly while dragging). Shield has limited energy on higher difficulties.
  3. Use the Wind Fan: hold on the field to charge, then release. Use ← ↑ ↓ → to aim the gust. Blow against the route to slow objects.
  4. Helper Drone regroups balloons (capacity & target in Settings) and clears hazards around them.
  5. Bubble gives nearby balloons a temporary colored shield. Choose your color in Settings.
  6. Difficulty increases balloons & hazards. Deliver as many as you can before the escort ends.

✨ ColorShift Cascades ✨

Guide the grid into a glowing color harmony.

Board: 3×3 · Easy
Moves: 0 · Chains: 0 · Harmony: 0% (best 0%)

How to Play 🎨

  1. Each square on the grid is a color node with its own hue.
  2. Click a node to shift its color. Adjacent nodes will also shift.
  3. The goal is to create a cascading color harmony—turn the board into matching or complementary hues.
  4. Special tiles:
    • Locked nodes cannot be shifted.
    • Mirror nodes reflect the color shift across the grid.
  5. Use your tools wisely:
    • Undo reverts your last move.
    • Chromatic Bomb randomizes a small area.
    • Spectrum Sweep shuffles an entire row’s colors.
  6. Choose different board sizes and difficulty levels to increase the challenge.
  7. Optional: turn on a guide voice and tap “🔊 Hint” to hear spoken suggestions.
  8. Each move counts! The fewer moves and the higher the harmony, the better your run.
  9. Try the Daily Challenge for one special board each day.
  10. Share your score or invite friends to see who can reach the highest harmony.
Tip: Focus on nodes that touch many neighbors to trigger satisfying chain reactions!

🌈 How to Play ColorShift Cascades

Tap a color node to nudge its hue. Its neighbors shift with it, and mirror nodes echo your move elsewhere on the grid.

  • Locked nodes cannot be changed.
  • Mirror nodes echo your move across the grid.
  • Use Undo, Chromatic Bomb, and Spectrum Sweep if you feel stuck.
  • Watch the Harmony percentage – higher is better!

How to Play LUMIN GATE

Goal: Rotate glowing sigils and weave light threads so each rune tile at the bottom only receives threads from sigils whose rotated symbol matches that rune’s symbol.

Basic Controls

  • Move a sigil: Click and drag a sigil to reposition it in the aurora field.
  • Rotate a sigil: Hold Shift and drag around the sigil (or use right-click + drag on some devices) to rotate it.
  • Weave a thread: Hold Alt, then click and drag from a sigil toward a rune tile to create or remove a light thread.

Winning the Gate

  • Each rune tile should have at least one thread attached.
  • All threads going into a rune must come from sigils whose rotated symbol matches that rune’s symbol.
  • When every rune satisfies those rules, press Check to solve the Gate.

Light, XP & Difficulty

  • Solving Gates earns you Light and XP — faster solves earn more.
  • Recent solve times adjust the difficulty automatically in Adaptive mode.
  • Your best time and currency are saved locally on this device.

Tools

  • 🔍 Reveal Hint (10 Light): Highlights a shard that needs attention.
  • 🧵 Auto-Thread (15 Light): Weaves a helpful thread between a shard and a rune.
  • 🜂 Stabilize (20 Light): Rotates a shard so it better fits its connected runes.
  • 🌫 Remove Noise (12 Light): Removes a thread that’s carrying the wrong symbol into a rune.

Invites & Sharing

  • Use your invite code to let friends join and earn bonus Light when a code is redeemed.
  • Share your score or a gate image using the Share buttons on the right panel.

Tip: Start by stabilizing one rune at a time — match its symbol, clear bad threads, then move on.

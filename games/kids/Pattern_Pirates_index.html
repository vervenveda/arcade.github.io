How to Play (Quick)
Step-by-step
  1. Look at the Map Card (right). It shows the sequence of tiles to visit in order.
  2. Move the pirate by tapping an adjacent square (or use arrow keys). Each move uses one move from the budget.
  3. Step onto tiles that match the next Map Card item (shape + color). When you match them all you finish the round and earn points.
  4. Collect power-ups by stepping on a tile with a power-up badge (🌉 🦜 ↩️). They appear randomly on the board.
  5. Open the Power-ups box to use them:
    • Bridge (🌉) — activate, then your next move can jump two squares orthogonally (skip one square). Use to reach distant targets quickly.
    • Parrot (🦜) — reveal positions of the next two goal tiles (they flash to show where to go).
    • Rewind (↩️) — rewinds the last move (restores your previous position, moves left, and goal progress).
  6. If you get stuck, use a Parrot hint or Rewind an earlier move. Undo (u) is also available for a simple step-back.
  7. Saves/loads are stored locally in your Mini Cloud. Everything stays on your device.

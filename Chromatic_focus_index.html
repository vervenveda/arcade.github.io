Chromatic Focus

A reflex & selective-attention game designed to measure cognitive filtering, reaction time, and decision accuracy.

How This Game Works

Colored bubbles will rise from the bottom of the screen. Each bubble displays a color name. Your task is to click ONLY the bubbles that match the current target color.

This game tests selective attention, visual processing speed, and impulse control under increasing cognitive load.

  • 🎯 Click bubbles that match the target color.
  • 🚫 Ignore all other colors — clicking them lowers your score.
  • ⏱ Faster correct reactions earn more points.
  • 🧠 Higher levels introduce faster speeds and broader color sets.
